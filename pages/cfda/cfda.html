CFDA/VOGUE Fashion Fund by Amazon

Vedio making for CFDA/VOGUE fashion fund. The Council of Fashion Designers of America and Vogue have created an initiative to support emerging American fashion talent. To rattle off past winners of the CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund is to list the biggest names in American fashion. Alexander Wang, Altuzarra, and Proenza Schoulerhave all taken home the main prize, with runners-up including the likes of Thom Browne, Rodarte, and 3.1 Phillip Lim. This video is a promotive documentary for CFDA/Vogue 2016 for the audience to understand and appreciate their funding program. It was released during Vogue’s CFDA/Vogue VIP event November 2015.
