CONDÉ NAST REPOSITION2016
—
Create Connect Condé Nast

Re-positioning campaign for Condé Nast and all its iconic titles. Campaign launched via social media with a tagline #creativityis and was promoted by celebrity brand ambassadors, ranging from Karlie Kloss, Kerry Washington, Idris Elba and Justin Beiber. Developed editorial concept for the video campaign, and have designed all the print ads —published by 13 brands, including Allure, Bon Appétit, Glamour, GQ, The New Yorker, Vogue, W— and outdoor ads which take over a Grand Central,NY. Video series are continously published on Facebook and Instagram.
